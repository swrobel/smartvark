What's a Smartvark Anyway?

Imagine getting offers for products and services that actually matter to you. No more spam emails, junk mail, and clipping through the Sunday paper. That’s the vision behind Smartvark.

From the very beginning, we set out to create a user friendly, personalized discount experience; one that understands what you are looking for. We’re shoppers too and we hate getting spammed with coupons and offers that we don’t care about, and we’re pretty sure you feel the same.

While the other guys focus on emailing offers they believe shoppers might like, we’re dedicated to delivering the ones you demand. By simply rating several offers with a thumbs up or thumbs down, we learn what you are looking for. Then we present you with relevant deals that save you the time you’d spend sifting through the junk.

People love smartvark because we introduce them to new places to shop, eat, and have fun in their community. We are all about supporting local businesses. Sure, there’s probably a lot of money to be made chasing after big box stores, but smartvark is more interested in introducing shoppers to the hidden gems in their neighborhood. Go ahead and give it a try.
